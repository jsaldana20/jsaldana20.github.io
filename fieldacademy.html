Borneo 2017

During my time in Borneo, I worked with a marine conservation center, saw orangutangs, went zip lining from one island to the next, and explored the city and culture of Borneo. The most interesting part of this trip was exploring the markets and swimming in the bright turquiose water. What also was fascinating was that I snorkeled for the first time and I had so much fun. A challenge for me was being there for 17 days without my family, which was by far the longest time I've been away from them, but I easily overcame that challenge. This was my first field academy and I really enjoyed my time there!

Zimbabwe 2018

During my time in Borneo, I helped out this organization called Hoops 4 Hope, which helps to give kids a safe envrionment to play so that they don't end up in the streets, I also went to a wildlife conservation center where I saw animals like elephants and rhinos, and I went to Victoria Falls! The most interesting part of this trip was when we would play basketball games with the kids in the schools. It was so much fun to be able to play a sport in a different country and to have fun with all this kids. A challenge for me was always waking up at 6-7, but my alarm helped me overcome this challenge. This was by far my favorite trip and I hope to one day return to Zimbabwe so that I can help out with the coaches and the amazing kids there.
