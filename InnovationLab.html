What is Innovation Lab?

The Innovation Lab @ Ross is an academy at the Ross School that is designed for people who have interests in science, mathematics, engineering, media, and technology. This program enables student to conduct independent projects that undertake a certain field of their interests.

My plans for the next two weeks are to finish making my logo designs on photoshop and order a sample navy blue hoodie to experiment with my designs.
